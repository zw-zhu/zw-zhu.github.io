About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Tongji University. Previously, I was a Boya Postdoc in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Peking University, working with Professor Zhiyu Tian at BICMR. I received my Ph.D. degree in Mathematics at the University of Utah under the supervision of Professor Tommaso de Fernex in 2020. I got my Bachelor's and Master's Degree in Mathematics at Fudan University.
